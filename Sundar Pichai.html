My role model

Sundai Pichai was born July 12, 1972, Chennai Tamil Nadu in India He is an Indian-born American computer scientist and also executive who worked for both Google, Inc and its holding company, Alphabet Inc. (2019).

He earned a degree in metallurgy - B.Tech 1993 and a silver medal at the IIT - Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, he was awarded a scholarship to study at the Stanford University (M.S. in engineering and materials science in 1995.

He remained in the United States thereafter working briefly for Applied Materials before joining Google in 2004 as the head of products management and development.

He started working on Google Toolbar which later enabled the use of Internet explorer and mozilla firefox web browsers and we got the easily accessible Google search engine. Over the course of work he released the same in the year 2008. In the same year Pichai was named vice-president of product development and he became more active in the public role of Google.

On August 10, 2015 he was selected to become the next CEO of Google and finally assumed the position on October 24, 2015.
